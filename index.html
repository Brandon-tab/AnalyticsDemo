Frequently Asked Questions

  1. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a platform for film enthusiasts to explore cinema through screenings, discussions, themed events, and more.
  • Membership is currently free! Just fill out the registration form (link provided) or contact an admin to join. If any fees (e.g., venue costs) apply in the future, we will notify members in advance.
  • We typically host offline or online events weekly/monthly. Exact schedules are announced via group chat/website/email.

Activities include but are not limited to: themed screenings, director retrospectives, post-screening discussions, guest lectures, and group trips to film festivals.

  • You can submit ideas via WeChat/email/post-event polls. We prioritize suggestions based on majority interest.

Offline events usually take place at the library (address provided). Online meeting links are shared in the member group before events.
